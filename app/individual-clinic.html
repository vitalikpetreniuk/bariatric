Augusta Live Healthy MD
Live Healthy MD is a comprehensive weight loss surgery and medical weight loss program in Georgia specializing in the treatment of the obesity epidemic. We opened in 2011 in the beautiful garden city, Augusta, Georgia. Since opening, our team of surgeons have worked tirelessly to build the most advanced weight loss program in the southeastern United States. We are now affiliated with Doctors Specialists - Bariatrics and Surgical, allowing us to take our weight loss program to the next level.
